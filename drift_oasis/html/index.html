    Welcome  To drift oasis

    How does it work?

    As you drift you get awarded money that you can spend in the vehicle shop.
    You can loose your money if you are drifting and hit something or someone.
    You will only receive money for drifts if you are going over 35KM/H.
    You can buy cars in the store, unlock more cars by upgrading your rank.
    You can drift on multiple maps, upgrading your rank will remove the fees.
